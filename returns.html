RETURNS & EXCHANGE POLICY

We care about our customers and stand by the quality of our products. If you’re not fully satisfied with your purchase, we offer an easy Returns & Exchange Policy to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Eligibility for Returns & Exchange

Items can be returned or exchanged within 7 days from the date of delivery.

Products must be in original condition: unused, unwashed, unaltered, untampered, and in saleable condition with original tags, invoice, and packaging intact (e.g., shoes must be returned with the original shoe box).

All products will undergo quality check upon receipt. Items failing the quality check will be shipped back to you.

Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

Non-Returnable / Non-Exchangeable Items

Gift cards, vouchers, and First Citizen Cards.

Altered or customized garments.

Innerwear, lingerie, and nightwear (for hygiene reasons).

Products purchased during sale can only be exchanged during the same sale period.

Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Product

If you receive a damaged, defective, or wrong product, you may request a Return/Exchange within the specified days. The product must be in original packaging and unused condition.

Ways to Return or Exchange
Through Our Courier Pickup

Log in to your account and raise a request under My Account > Orders > Return/Exchange.

Or email us at customercare@fabrinique.com with order details.

Our courier will collect the item within 2 working days of your request.

Please retain the SMS confirmation or pickup receipt for reference.

Through Self-Courier

Send the product directly to our warehouse/store address mentioned on the invoice.

Ensure the product is packed securely with original tags and invoice.

Refund & Exchange Timeline

Return/Exchange requests are processed within 7–10 working days after the product passes quality check.

Refunds are initiated as per the mode selected during return request and cannot be modified later.

Important Notes

Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective or wrong product.

Loyalty points earned on returned products will be reversed.
