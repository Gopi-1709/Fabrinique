DELIVERY POLICY

We ensure a smooth and timely delivery process for all your orders. Please read the following terms carefully to understand our delivery process and timelines.

Dispatch & Shipping

The expected dispatch time is mentioned on each product’s description page.

All shipments (including gifts) will include a tax invoice as per Indian tax regulations.

A minimum shipping fee of ₹50 is charged for orders equal to or below ₹499. This will be added during checkout before completing your purchase.

Currently, we do not deliver outside India.

Courier Partners

We deliver products through trusted courier partners:

Bluedart Courier (www.bluedart.com)

Delhivery Courier (www.delhivery.com)

Ecom Express (www.ecomexpress.in)

Modes of Delivery
Standard Delivery

Delivered within 3–5 working days from order date.

Express Store Pick-Up (if applicable)

Self pick-up from selected store locations.

Note: If products are procured from outside your state, delivery may take 7–10 working days. For multiple products in an order, items may ship separately, and updates will be sent via email.

Delivery Issues

If we cannot deliver your order completely, we will cancel the undelivered part and initiate a refund to your original payment mode.

For prepaid payments, refunds are processed to the original payment method.

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds are not applicable.

Damaged / Tampered Packages

If the outer packaging is tampered or damaged at the time of delivery, do not accept the package. Inform us at customercare@fabrinique.com immediately.

If you notice damage/defects after accepting delivery, email us with images of the product and packaging within 2 working days of receiving delivery confirmation (SMS or email). Requests made beyond this period will not be accepted.

Non-Delivery Claims

If you claim non-receipt of your order, notify us within 2 working days from delivery confirmation. Claims beyond this period will not be accepted.

Return to Origin (RTO)

If the courier partner is unable to deliver within the stipulated time, the shipment will be returned to us.

Refunds for prepaid orders will be initiated to the original payment method. COD orders are not eligible for refunds.
