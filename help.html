Help / Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to the Fabrinique Help Center. We’ve listed answers to the most common questions about shopping, orders, payments, delivery, and returns.

Orders & Payments
Q1: How can I place an order?

You can browse our products, add items to your cart, and complete your purchase by providing your details and choosing a payment method at checkout.

Q2: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) – Available on select orders
Q3: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or canceled before dispatch. Please email us at customercare@fabrinique.com with your order ID as soon as possible.

Shipping & Delivery
Q4: How long will it take to receive my order?
  • Standard Delivery: 3–5 working days.
  • In some cases (e.g., out-of-state procurement), delivery may take 7–10 working days.
Q5: Do you charge for shipping?

A shipping fee of ₹50 is applied for orders of ₹499 or below. Orders above ₹499 enjoy free shipping.

Q6: Do you deliver outside India?

Currently, we do not offer international delivery.

Returns & Exchanges
Q7: What is your return policy?

We accept returns and exchanges within the specified number of days (as mentioned on the product page), provided the items are unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags intact.

Q8: How do I initiate a return or exchange?

You can raise a request via:

  • My Account > Orders > Return/Exchange (if you are a registered user)
  • Or email us at customercare@fabrinique.com with your order details
Q9: Are there items that cannot be returned?

Yes, returns are not accepted for:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and nightwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Altered or customized garments.
  • Gift cards and vouchers.
Refunds
Q10: How are refunds processed?

Refunds are issued to the original payment method once the returned item passes quality checks. For COD orders, refunds are not applicable.

Q11: How long does it take to get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 7–10 working days after the product is received and inspected.

Account & Support
Q12: Do I need to create an account to shop?

No, you can shop as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders and manage returns easily.

Q13: How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us at:

  • Email: customercare@fabrinique.com
  • Operating Hours: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Mon–Sat)
General
Q14: Are the product colors exactly as shown?

We strive to display colors accurately, but slight variations may occur due to screen settings or lighting.

Q15: Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, gift wrapping is available at checkout for select items.
